How it works

Simple.
No surprises.

Working with international clients remotely means one thing: clear communication at every step. Here's exactly what happens when we work together.

01
Brief & Scope

We talk — by email, WhatsApp, or video call. You tell me what you need, your timeline, and your budget. I tell you honestly if I'm the right fit and what I can deliver.

02
Proposal & Quote

I send a detailed proposal within 24–48 hours: scope, deliverables, timeline, and a clear price. No hidden fees. 50% upfront to book the slot, 50% on final delivery.

03
Production

I work. You get progress updates at key milestones — not radio silence. First draft delivered on schedule, with a revision round included in every project.

04
Delivery

Final files delivered in the formats you need — broadcast-ready video, vector source files, brand assets in all sizes. Clean handoff, no confusion about what you're getting.
